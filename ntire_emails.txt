Our method, "Championship SR," is a multi-expert fusion approach that combines four frozen pre-trained SR models (DRCT-L, GRL-B, NAFNet-64, and MambaIR) through a lightweight trainable fusion network (~1.2M parameters). The fusion is guided by a 9-band multi-domain frequency decomposition (DCT + DWT + FFT) and uses hierarchical attention with large kernel attention (k=21) for global spatial context. We also use a difficulty-adaptive gating mechanism that dynamically routes expert contributions based on local image complexity.
